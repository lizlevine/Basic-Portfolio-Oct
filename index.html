About Me

Hi! My name is Liz Levine, and I'm originally from Detroit, Michigan. I've been living in Manhattan since the 1980's when the city looked and felt very different than it does today. I've have the priviledge to explore several career paths and develop many interests and hobbies. I began working in publishing, then entered the fashion industry working in the luxury market sector buying, selling & merchandising. I spent more than a decade in the interior design industry collaborating with the architects and designers on commercial, hospitality and high end residential projects.

Like many New Yorkers, I found the urban life very demanding. This is when I discovered a passion for the then unknown, Pilates Method. I was blessed to study directly under the renowned Romana Kryzanowska (the only living decendent and apprentice to Joseph Pilates).

Today, I spend much of my free time with Reiki, my two-year-old beloved Basenji who is pictured here as a 12-week-old puppy. He comes from a long line of AKC Grand Champions and is following in his ancestor's paws, competing for the champion title. Much of my free time is spent training and grooming him for the show ring!
