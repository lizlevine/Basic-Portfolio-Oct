About Me

Hi! I'm originally from Detroit, Michigan hand have been living in Manhattan since the 1980's. I've have had several careers and many interests and hobbies. I began in book publishing, then went on to fashion working in the luxury market sector sector as a buying, selling and merchandising ready-to-wear and accessories. I've also worked with the architectural and design communities on commercial and high end residential projects.

Like many New Yorkers, I found the urban live very stressful and found little time to care for my physical health and well being. This is when I discovered my passion for pilates and ultimately studied with the renowned Romana K., a direct decendent and apprentice to Joseph Pilates.

Today, I spend much of my free time with my beloved 2 year old Basenji who is pictured here with my husband. He comes from a long line of AKC Grand Champions and is following his ancestors campaigning for the title. I spent a lot of time training and grooming him for the show ring.

If you are interested in learning more about the exquisite and ancient Basenji breed, please visit my Passion "Pet" Project here.
